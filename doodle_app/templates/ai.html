Quick, Draw! Dataset

We utilize the Google Quick, Draw! Dataset, a massive collection of 50 million hand-drawn sketches across 345 categories. This diverse and extensive dataset enables our machine learning model to recognize a wide range of doodles with high accuracy.

Neural Network Architecture

Convolutional Layers

Multiple convolutional layers extract intricate features from doodle inputs, capturing line patterns, shapes, and structural nuances.

Pooling Layers

Reduces spatial dimensions while retaining essential image characteristics, improving computational efficiency.

Fully Connected Layers

Combines extracted features to make final classification decisions across 345 drawing categories.
